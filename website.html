White Kalakand

A popular Indian sweet treat, White Kalakand is a creamy, milky delight made with:

  • Fresh milk
  • Sugar
  • Chenna (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Cardamom powder (for flavor)

Taste & Flavor:

White Kalakand boasts a:

  • Rich, creamy texture
  • Sweet, milky flavor
  • Subtle tanginess from the chenna
  • Hint of cardamom's warm, aromatic spice
  • Smooth, velvety mouthfeel

This sweet treat is often served chilled, making it a refreshing dessert or snack perfect for any occasion!

Brown Kalakand

A roasted variation of the classic White Kalakand, Brown Kalakand is a rich, caramelized delight made by roasting White Kalakand to perfection, bringing out a deeper flavor and texture.


Taste & Flavor:

Brown Kalakand boasts a:

  • Deep, caramelized flavor
  • Nutty, roasted taste
  • Denser, creamier texture than White Kalakand
  • Hint of burnt sugar, adding a smoky note
  • A sweet, indulgent treat with a velvety mouthfeel

The roasting process gives Brown Kalakand a distinctive flavor and texture, setting it apart from its white counterpart. It's a perfect treat for those who love a deeper, more caramelized taste!
